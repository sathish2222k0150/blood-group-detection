Blood Type Overview

Type A

  • Strong immune system
  • Lower risk of heart disease
  • Sensitive digestive system
  • Higher stress levels
  • Better response to vegetarian diets

Type B

  • Strong metabolism
  • Balanced immune system
  • Flexible dietary needs
  • Good stress management
  • Resistant to many diseases

Type AB

  • Universal plasma donor
  • Adaptable digestive system
  • Unique immune characteristics
  • Higher cognitive function
  • Complex dietary requirements

Type O

  • Universal donor
  • Strong physical endurance
  • Efficient digestion
  • Natural leadership traits
  • Higher metabolism rate
